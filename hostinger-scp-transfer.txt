SECURE COPY PROTOCOL (SCP)
---

--- Hostinger SCP Transfer
    -- Description: Copy files from your local machine to hostinger

    -- Prerequisite
      -- A valid Hostinger hosting account

    -- Usage:
       -- port: 65002
       -- Open terminal
       -- command:
          -- scp -P 65002 ./path/to/file username@[ftp-ip-address]:/home/[username]/domains/[domain]/public_html
       -- Enter password
       -- Hit Enter
       -- Done
